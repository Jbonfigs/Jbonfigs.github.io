*EECE 7352: Computer Architecture — October 2025 to December 2025*

Investigated fault injection attacks on AES-GCM authenticated encryption. By inducing targeted hardware faults during the GCM authentication tag computation, the project demonstrated how fault injection can break authentication guarantees even when the underlying AES cipher remains intact. Explored both attack methodology and potential architectural mitigations.
