*EECE 5698: FPGAs in the Cloud — October 2025 to December 2025*

Designed and implemented an FPGA-accelerated Monte Carlo simulation for pricing options under the Heston stochastic volatility model. The project used High-Level Synthesis (HLS) to target a cloud FPGA, exploiting hardware parallelism to accelerate the simulation compared to a CPU baseline.
