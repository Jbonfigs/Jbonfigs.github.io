I am a first-year PhD student in Computer Engineering at Northeastern University, advised by [Dr. Francesco Restuccia](https://coe.northeastern.edu/people/restuccia-francesco/) in the [MENTIS Lab](https://mentis.info/). My research focuses on fault injection attacks and resilient FPGA design for convolutional neural networks.

I completed my B.S. in Computer Engineering and Mathematical Sciences at Binghamton University in May 2024.

---

## Research

**PhD Research — MENTIS Lab, Northeastern University** *(May 2025 – Present)*  
Working under Dr. Francesco Restuccia on fault injection and resilient FPGA design for CNNs. Research explores how hardware-level faults can compromise neural network inference and how to build robust defenses at the architecture level.

**Undergraduate Research Assistant — Binghamton University** *(January 2024 – June 2024)*  
Worked under Professor JingZhou Zhao. 

**TFHE for Embedded IoT Applications** *(January 2024 – May 2024)*  
Collaborated with a Binghamton University faculty member and their PhD student to explore the implementation of Fully Homomorphic Encryption over the Torus (TFHE) for resource-constrained embedded IoT devices.

**Autonomously Radio-Controlled Racing Car with AI — REU Program** *(2023 – May 2024)*  
Member of a 15+ person team developing a third-person robot coach and autonomous RC race car capable of acquiring vehicle telemetry in real-time, coaching human racers, and competing autonomously. Focused on the Arduino Nano 33 BLE, researching direct memory access to efficiently sample steering, throttle, motor current, RPM, temperature, and voltage data over Bluetooth to a Jetson Xavier. Project completed May 2024.
